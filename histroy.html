Our Journey

Every cup has a past.

2019

Where it all began

The café started as a 10-seat space with one espresso machine and a commitment to quality over profit.

2020

Surviving the hard times

Despite lockdowns, we adapted with takeaway service and local delivery — no compromises made.

2022

Growing the community

Expanded seating, introduced subscriptions, and built a loyal regular base.

2024

Where we are now

A modern café focused on consistency, experience, and long-term relationships.
